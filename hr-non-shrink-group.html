hr-non-shrink-group

Our CG-700 is made of cement, special additives, and aggregates. It is a special type of cementitious material used in various construction applications. It can resist impact, vibration, oil, and fire. It offers several benefits and is commonly used for the following purposes:

  • Precision machinery installation ensuring proper alignment, reducing vibrations, and minimizing the risk of shifting or movement during operations.
  • Helps improve steel quality by avoiding pickup in steel.
  • Repair and rehabilitation of cracks, voids, and other defects in concrete structures.
  • Improves thermal insulation near the Taphole area which helps the furnace maintain the desired temperature.
  • Grouted reinforcements to fill the voids between rebar and the surrounding concrete giving it a tighter bond.
  • Anchoring bolts, dowels, and other embedded elements in concrete which prevents loosening due to dynamic loads or environmental factors.
  • Overall, our CG-700 is valued for its high strength, minimal shrinkage, and excellent load-bearing properties, making it suitable for a wide range of applications in construction, repair, and infrastructure projects.

Salient Features:

  • 1. Non-Shrink
  • 2. Free Flowing
  • 3. Self-Leveling
  • 4. Expansive
  • 5. Earl High Strength
  • 6. Suitable Even in Hot Atmosphere
  • 7. Spreads Uniformly in All Direction Without Segregation

General Instructions for Installation:

  • 1. Add 13-15% water as prescribed. Mix four to five minutes.
  • 2. Hand mixing or concrete mixing both would do for this grout.
  • 3. Mix till you find bubbles in grout.
  • 4. The grout is ready for placement. It would flow freely in all direction.
  • 5. Using of vibrator and rodding would boost flow of grout.
  • 6. Allow grouted area to be cured for 72 hours.
