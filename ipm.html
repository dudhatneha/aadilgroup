About Imperial pack masterz

A leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality refractory products in India.

Established in 2003, Hazira Refractory Works Pvt. Ltd. has over two decades of experience in manufacturing and supplying of quality refractory products. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we have a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Surat, Gujarat, India, equipped with reliable machinery and technology to produce high-quality products that meet the needs of our customers.

At Hazira Refractory Works Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to provide our customers the best of quality refractory products at competitive prices. We understand the importance of refractory products in various industries and strive to deliver products that meet the highest standards of quality, durability, and performance.

We offer comprehensive range of refractory products including, Conventional and Dense Castable, Insulating Castable, Gunning Castable, Low Cement & Ultra Low Cement Castable, Resin Bonded Castable, Ramming & Gunning Masses, EBT Filling Mass, Back Filling Mass, Nozzle Filling Compound, Tundish Spray, Vibro Mass, Precast Shapes (PCPF) and other refractory products. Our products are widely used in Steel, Power, Cement, Ferrous, Non-Ferrous, Petro Chemical Plants. and other industries.

Corrugated packaging industry in deep trouble as raw material prices shoot up

Corrugated packaging industry has landed in deep trouble as prices of its principal raw material - kraft paper have started shooting up over the past few days. There has been a sharp price increase of Rs 5000 per tonne of kraft paper within a span of 10 days. The mills say that this is not the end and expect further increase in the coming days, said Federation of Corrugated Box Manufacturers of India (FCBM) in a release issued on Monday.
