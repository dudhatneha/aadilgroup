Hazira Packaging & Engineering Services

About Hazira Packaging & Engineering Service

We offer comprehensive packaging services within polyester plants, covering the entire process and operations. From the initial stages of extrusion and additive mixing to the final steps of coiling and recycling, our team is equipped to handle all aspects of packaging for 4G and 5G infrastructure projects, particularly for Reliance Jio.

With a focus on quality and efficiency, we ensure that every stage of the process is executed with precision and attention to detail. Our expertise in extrusion and additive mixing allows us to create high-quality HDPE ducts that meet the stringent requirements of 4G and 5G networks.

In addition, our coiling operations are optimized for reliability and consistency, ensuring that the ducts are neatly wound and ready for deployment in telecom infrastructure projects. We also prioritize sustainability by implementing recycling operations to minimize waste and maximize resource utilization.

At every step of the way, we adhere to industry best practices and quality standards to deliver packaging solutions that meet the needs of our clients, including Reliance Jio. By partnering with us, clients can rest assured that their packaging requirements will be handled efficiently and effectively, enabling them to focus on their core business objectives.

We also provide forklifts with operators for various operations.
