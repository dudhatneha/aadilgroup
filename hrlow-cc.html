HR Mid-Purity Conventional Castable

Features

  • Versatility: These castables are designed to be versatile, allowing for a wide range of applications in various industries.
  • Cost-Effective: With its balanced composition and performance characteristics, these castables provide a cost-effective solution for refractory linings without compromising on quality and reliability.
  • Versatility:Mechanical Strength: Medium-duty castables have a higher proportion of refractory aggregates, which provides them with improved mechanical strength and density compared to lightweight castables. This allows them to withstand physical stresses and structural loads in industrial applications.

Mid-purity conventional castable refers to a type of refractory material that is used for lining low to mid-temperature industrial equipment where the heat impact ranges from 1000°C to 1600°C. They offer a balance between cost, performance, thermal insulation and durability, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications that require moderate refractory performance. Typically having low Alumina contentment of below 60%.
